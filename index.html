New York City Subway System

History Stations Graffiti
The New York City subway system, an iconic emblem of urban mobility and design, stands as one of the largest and most famous public transportation networks in the world. Its inception and evolution are deeply intertwined with the city's history, reflecting New York's growth and its pivotal role in shaping urban public transit. This narrative explores the origins, development, and cultural significance of the NYC subway, offering a lens through which to view the broader historical and social dynamics of urban America.
